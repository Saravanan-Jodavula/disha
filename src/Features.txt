App Features  
1. My Community 
   Depending on the Branch and the passout year, disha should be able to display it's 
   users, all their respective branch mates in a so called "My Community List"  
2. Announcements Section 
   Place where alumni can share important information
3. disha-Spaces 
   Alumni can create their own custom manageable groups that they can send invites to add 
   members, and only the members of the Space will be notified with the info shared in 
   the space.
